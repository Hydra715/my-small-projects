Elon Musk

The man who wonders about living on Mars

Here's a timeline of Mr.Elon Musk life:

  • 1971 - Born in Pretoria, South Africa

  • 1980 - Parents Divorce

  • 1981 - Interest in Computing

  • 1983 - Sold his first video game, Blastar

  • 1989 - Moved to Canada to attend Queen's University

  • 1992 - Went to University of Pennsylvania to Study Business and Physics

  • 1995 - Started Zip2, a Web Software Company

  • 1999 - Sold Zip2 to Compaq for US$307 million

  • 1999 - Cofounded X.com, a financial service and payment company

  • 2000 - X.com merged with Confinity to form PayPal, Musk married his first wife

  • 2000 - Musk got Fired from PayPal

  • 2001 - Interest in Space Exploration

  • 2001 - Went to Moscow to buy ICBMs to send payloads to Mars

  • 2002 - Got an idea to build affordable Rockets

  • 2002 - Founded SpaceX, a space exploration company

  • 2002 - eBay acquired PayPal

  • 2003 - Tesla, Inc was incorporated

Failure is an option here. If things are not failing, you are not innovating enough. - Elon Musk
